Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How to get started with us?

ShawnSMS.com was created with a simple interface, to help users navigate the site easily. By using ShawnSMS.com you can verify any Website or Application like Facebook, Instagram, Tinder, POF, WhatsApp, etc in just a few minutes. Just Register and Top UP Your account.

How can I Top-Up?

With the help of Paystack you can add funds via Bank Transfer, Visa/Mastercard, and USSD. Once this is done, the system adds the funds automatically to your account.

How do I buy the numbers and get the code?
  • Select a country of your choice, select a service like (Twitter, telegram or WhatsApp, etc.), click on the “place orders” button, and a number will be generated. You can also search for a service using the search box provided.
  • Copy the number generated and paste it on the platform of the services(Twitter, telegram or WhatsApp, etc.)
  • After some time normally btw 30 seconds to 7 minutes an OTP code from the service provider will be generated for you.
Do I have to be online whenever I place an order?

YES, you have to be online so that you can receive your OTP swiftly on the site.

Why can't I get a number?

You might experience a delay in getting your number because we have a lot of users trying to buy that particular service at that moment, kindly exercise patience and your number will be delivered to you in no time.
